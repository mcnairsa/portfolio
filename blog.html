Hero & CTA

Two distinct but related aspects of web page design. A good landing page will included both.

The Hero Area/Section ...

... is an arresting image or piece of text that grabs your visitor's attention. It is what makes the visitor stay on your site to explore further. A strong Hero Area is key to a successful site whatever your measure of success may be. stfw [Google or DuckduckGo or other engine of choice, Hey! it's a free planet! ... web design hero area/section]

The Call-to-Action ...

... is what makes your site work. It is what gets the visitor to sign up, buy your product or engage with you.

Very often the CTA employs a button. It should allow the user/visitor to engage in whatever way you want hem to with the minimum of effort.

These two, the Hero Section and the Call to Action often work together with the Hero Area including a cta button.
